About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher specializing in robotics and machine learning, with a focus on robot navigation. I am currently pursuing my honours year research on enhancing robot navigation through the integration of world models, reinforcement learning, and vision-language models. My work centers on enabling embodied agents to understand, reason about, and interact with complex environments.

Research Interests

I am particularly motivated by the challenge of bridging perception, memory, and decision-making to create more capable autonomous agents. My current areas of exploration include:

  • LLM agents
  • Vision-language navigation
  • World models
  • Robot navigation and contextual reasoning
